Welcome to the society

Who are we?

We are a society of students, young professionals and any other stray socks in the community trying to find their way in life. Whether it is to get your fabrics clean and fresh, or to enjoy a cuppa while detoxing your ears with some Friday open mic nights, come along and join the society!
