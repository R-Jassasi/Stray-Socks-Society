Welcome to the society

Who are we?

We are a society of students, young professionals and other stray socks in the community trying to find their way in life. Whether it is to get your fabrics clean and fresh, to enjoy a cuppa while detoxing your ears with some Friday open mic nights, or even to find socks that match you, come along and join the society!

launderette

Nothing beats a clean sock fresh as a daisy

Our store is equipped with modern, environmentally friendly washing machines, we want you to have clean socks while keeping nature clean too! They use less water and electricity while getting the job done as best as it can be. They are also people friendly and easy to use with instructions and staff around always happy to help. We offer a list of washing, drying, ironing and dry cleaning services all priced reasonably even for students. We also have memberships and discounts, please ask the staff in store for more details.

We offer washing services for busy people, too. Just drop off your clothes and we will do them for you. If you have a hotel, restaurant or like business, please get in touch and ask about the services we provide and our prices. We are sure we will get you a fantastice deal :)
